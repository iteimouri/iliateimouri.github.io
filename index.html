About

Greetings, my name is Ilia. I possess a Doctorate in Theoretical Physics, focusing on String Theory and Modified Theories of Gravity. Specifically, I have extensively researched various aspects of the Infinite Derivative Theory of Gravity.
After completing my PhD, I accepted a postdoctoral fellowship at the Boston Consulting Group (BCG) and the London Institute for Mathematical Sciences, where I focused on the mathematical structure of innovation. Subsequently, I served as a Scientific Consultant for BCG, guiding their COVID Pandemic Modelling Program. I then transitioned to a Data Science Fellowship at Faculty, followed by a role as a Data Scientist at IQVIA. Currently, I am a Senior Data Scientist at UBS, providing cutting-edge Artificial Intelligence solutions and products to various divisions of the bank.
In addition to my corporate pursuits, I am also a Co-Founder of the Nvesto Stock Exchange. Nvesto is an innovative platform that gamifies the stock market, allowing users to trade using cryptocurrencies to mitigate the risk associated with the cryptocurrency market's volatility. Furthermore, Nvesto also has a research aspect, which can be utilised to address various questions related to Behavioral Economics.
